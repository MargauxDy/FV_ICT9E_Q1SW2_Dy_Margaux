Characters in Noli Me Tangere

Noli Me Tangere is a book written by Jose Rizal. It was written about the Philippine society during the Spanish colonization. Jose Rizal also added his own personal experiences in this book. This book helped Filipinos realize the injustices done to them by the Spaniards.

Listed below are six characters from Noli Me Tangere.


Picture of Character Character Description
1. Crisostomo Ibarra
  • He is the main character of Noli Me Tangere and is based on the author, Jose Rizal.
  • He studied in Europe for 7 years before going back to the Philippines, where the story now takes place.
2. Maria Clara
  • She is the love interest of Crisostomo Ibarra.
  • Her aunt, Tiya Isabel, was the one who looked after her since her mother passed early.
  • She is described as very beautiful and eye-catching.
3. Don Rafael Ibarra
  • He is the father of Crisostomo Ibarra.
  • He was put in prison for something he didn't do and died there.
4. Kapitan Tiago
  • He is the father of Maria Clara.
  • He is a Filipino who has a high status due to his connections with Spanish priests.
5. Padre Damaso
  • He is the biological father of Maria Clara.
  • He is a Spanish priest who used to be best friends with Don Rafael Ibarra, but they had disagreements which made Padre Damaso bitter with Don Rafael.
6. Pia Alba
  • She is the mother of Maria Clara and wife of Kapitan Tiago.
  • She was having fertility issues, so she danced in Obando to pray for a baby. She became pregnant, but it was because Padre Damaso sexually assaulted her.
  • She died from stress and trauma shortly after Maria Clara was born.
